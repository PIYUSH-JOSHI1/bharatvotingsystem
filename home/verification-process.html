QR Verification Process

Your Unique QR Code

This QR code will be used for instant verification at the polling booth.

Download or take a screenshot of this QR code and bring it with you on election day.

How to Use Your QR Code

  1. Arrive at your designated polling booth with your QR code.
  2. Present the QR code to the verification officer.
  3. The officer will scan the code using the Bharat Voting System app.
  4. Your identity will be verified instantly.
  5. Proceed to cast your vote.

Benefits of QR Verification

  • Reduces waiting time by up to 70%
  • Enhanced security with multi-level encryption
  • Prevents identity fraud
  • Streamlines the voting process
  • Provides real-time verification status

Verification Process Steps

1

Registration

Create an account with your Aadhaar and personal details.

2

Verification

Your details are verified against electoral records.

3

4

5

Vote Casting

After verification, proceed to cast your vote securely.

Frequently Asked Verification Questions

What if I forget to bring my QR code on election day?

Don't worry! You can still vote by presenting your Voter ID card and other approved identification documents. However, this will require following the traditional verification process, which might take longer.

Is the QR code system secure?

Yes, the QR code uses multi-level encryption and is linked to your biometric data. Each QR code is unique and cannot be duplicated. Additionally, the verification process includes multiple security checks to prevent fraud.

Can someone else use my QR code to vote?

No, the QR verification is just the first step. At the polling booth, your identity will still be verified against your biometric data (fingerprint or iris scan) to ensure that only you can cast your vote.

How long is the QR code valid for?

The QR code is valid only for the specific election it was generated for. You'll need to generate a new QR code for each election through your Bharat Voting System account.
